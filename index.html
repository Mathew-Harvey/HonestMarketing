Our Philosophy: Simple Is Powerful

Raw HTML is amazing and just works.

Add a little CSS and it's beautiful. Sprinkle in some JavaScript only when you actually need it. That's it. That's the secret.

While everyone else is debugging their 47 dependencies, your site is already loaded. While they're explaining their "tech stack," you're making sales.

We believe in progressive enhancement, not progressive complications.
